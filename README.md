📊 Telco Customer Churn Analysis

Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges faced by telecommunication companies. This project analyzes customer behavior to identify the major factors driving churn and provides actionable business recommendations.

The project combines:

* Python (Data Cleaning & EDA)
* Power BI (Interactive Dashboard)
* Business Analytics
* Executive Reporting
* Business Presentation

⸻

Business Problem

Customer churn reduces revenue and increases customer acquisition costs.

The objective of this project is to discover:

* Why customers leave
* Which customers are most likely to churn
* What actions the business should take to improve retention

⸻

Key Insights

* Month-to-month contracts have the highest churn.
* Fiber Optic customers churn more frequently.
* Electronic Check customers are more likely to leave.
* Customers without Tech Support have significantly higher churn.
* Lack of Online Security increases churn risk.
* Customers with short tenure are the highest-risk group.

⸻

Business Recommendations

* Promote long-term contracts.
* Improve onboarding for new customers.
* Bundle Tech Support and Online Security.
* Encourage automatic payment methods.
* Target high-risk customer segments with retention campaigns.
